ABOUT ME!

Hi, everyonne! My name is Akansha Dey. I am a senior studying computer science at Michigan
State University and I love food. I was born in Kolkata, India and moved to Michigan with my parents
when I was 11. Although I love our evergreen Michigan, I do miss the bengali streetfood and my
grandmas special recipies a lot. This website is dedicated to all the foodies out there who moved
across the world and miss home a little too much sometimes.

We are going to talk about fun stuff

  • Food
  • Culture
  • Experiences growing up in a different country
  • Special memories

follow me along on my journey to my childhood and experience some of the fun, flavorful, delicious food.
